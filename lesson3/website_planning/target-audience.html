Target Audience

The target audience of the tuAfuera.com website is described below:

  • Who: Students, families, people that loves to go out and enjoy nature and also people that likes to be aware of weather conditions.
  • Age: All ages.
  • Income: Some of the information the website provides is about events that are free and some that are not free. The income goes from $10,000 and above annually.
