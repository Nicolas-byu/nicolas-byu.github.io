Target Audience

The target audience of the tuAfuera.com website is described below:

  • Who: Students, families, people that love to go out and enjoy nature, and also people that like to be aware of weather conditions.
  • Age: All ages.
  • Income: Some of the information the website provides is about events that are free and some that are not free. The income goes from $10,000 and above annually.

Personas

This is the Jackson family.

The Jackson family loves to go out of their house and enjoy nature. Sometimes they go out without knowing the weather and when they think the day is going to be sunny, the rain begins to fall. They need a website to tell them what type of weather is going to be in the specific area they are going to.

This is Harris.

Harris loves to go to outdoor events, but he is been feeling frustrated lately because when he goes to an event, the event gets canceled because of the weather condition. When he heard about our website he was very happy to hear that tuAfuera provides all the information about local events.
